A Homebound Individual is someone who spends a significant portion of their time at home without actively engaging in external activities or employment. While each person's circumstances may vary, some individuals may find themselves in this situation due to various reasons, such as health concerns, personal preferences, or life circumstances.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Self-Care

    Prioritize self-care and well-being.

  2. Mental Health

    Be aware of mental health and emotional well-being

  3. Household Management

    Take responsibility for the upkeep and organization of the home environment.
